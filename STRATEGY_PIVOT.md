# 🚀 CareerPilot Salone: Strategic Master Plan

## The Challenge
**Young People Choose Careers Blindly.**

1.  **No Career Guidance**
    *   Students finish secondary school or university without knowing which careers have demand or which skills employers actually want.
2.  **Scattered Opportunities**
    *   Jobs, scholarships, and trainings are spread across WhatsApp groups and Facebook posts — often shared too late.
3.  **Wrong Choices**
    *   Result: wasted years and money on wrong courses, high youth unemployment, and loss of confidence in the future.

---

## The Solution
**Your Personal Career Mentor in Your Pocket.**

CareerPilot Salone is an AI-powered career guidance platform delivered through **Web and WhatsApp**, designed specifically for Sierra Leonean youth. It acts like a personal mentor, providing data-backed guidance exactly when you need it.

### Three Core Functions
1.  **Discover the right career path** based on who you are.
2.  **Get step-by-step guidance** on what to study and where.
3.  **Connect to real opportunities** at the right time.

---

## The Target Audience (Built For Sierra Leone's Youth)
*   **Students & School Leavers (15–25):** Unsure what career to choose, need guidance on courses and universities. Mostly use WhatsApp.
*   **Graduates & Job Seekers (20–30):** Struggling to find jobs, unsure what skills they're missing, need CV help.
*   **Youth Organizations & NGOs:** Need a system to guide thousands of youths efficiently.

---

## The Journey: How It Works
1.  **Onboarding & Career Test:** User takes a short AI assessment covering interests, skills, and location.
2.  **AI Career Analysis:** Processes data to return top career matches with local market context (Demand/Salary).
3.  **University & Job Matching:** Links to relevant courses (FBC, IPAM, etc.) and entry-level roles.
4.  **Skill Roadmap:** A 3-month plan: "What to learn this week."
5.  **Opportunities & Alerts:** Real-time WhatsApp/Web alerts for jobs and scholarships.
6.  **CV & Career Support:** AI-powered CV building and skill positioning.

---

## 🟢 Why WhatsApp Changes Everything
**Inclusive & Accessible.**

*   **Most youths in Sierra Leone don't have laptops** and have limited data, but they use WhatsApp daily.
*   **Works everywhere:** Even in areas with limited connectivity.
*   **Zero learning curve:** Familiar interface everyone already knows.
*   **Maximum reach:** Connects with youth wherever they are.

*This is what makes CareerPilot Salone truly scalable and inclusive across Sierra Leone.*
