# 🚀 CareerPilot Salone: Strategic Pivot
**Focus: Solving the "Employability Function Gap" in Sierra Leone**

## 1. The Solid Real Problem
**The "CV Black Hole" & Trust Gap.**
*   **Job Seekers:** Thousands of FBC/IPAM graduates apply for jobs but get zero replies. Why? Their CVs are poorly formatted, lack keywords, and fail to highlight relevant skills. They are invisible.
*   **Employers:** Companies in Freetown are desperate for talent but drown in low-quality applications. They don't trust the paper degrees and rely on "who knows who" (nepotism) because they lack a reliable way to verify competence.

## 2. The Solution
**CareerPilot isn't just a "guidance site" anymore. It is a "Verified Professional Identity Platform."**
*   **For Users:** An AI-powered "Career Passport".
    *   Builds a perfect, Western-standard CV automatically (optimized for ATS).
    *   Verifies skills via quick in-app quizzes (e.g., "Excel Proficiency", "Customer Service").
*   **Value Prop:** "Don't just apply. **Get Verified. Get Hired.**"

## 3. Reference Target User
**"Mohamed the Graduate"**
*   **Profile:** 23 years old, final year at IPAM (Business Admin).
*   **Pain:** Has applied to 50 jobs at banks/NGOs with no response. Uses a generic CV template from a cyber café. Frustrated by "connection-based" hiring.
*   **Tech:** Has a smartphone (Tecno/Infinix), limited data. Needs a tool that works offline/low-data.

## 4. Business Model (Sustainable Revenue)
**Primary: B2B "Talent Access" (The LinkedIn/Recruiter Model)**
*   **Free for Users:** Building the CV and taking tests is 100% free (to acquire user base).
*   **Paid for Employers:** Companies pay to access the **"Verified Talent Pool"**.
    *   *Example:* "Find me 10 candidates with Verified Excel Skills in Freetown." -> $50 fee.
*   **Secondary: User Micro-Transactions (Orange Money)**
    *   *Premium Templates:* 10 SLE per download.
    *   *AI Cover Letter Gen:* 5 SLE per letter.

## 5. Implementation Roadmap (Pivot)
1.  **Homepage:** Rewrite to focus 100% on "Build your Professional CV & Get Verified."
2.  **CV Builder:** Enhance with "Verified Badges" (e.g., "English Certified", "IT Literate").
3.  **Employer Portal:** Simple page for companies to "Request Talent."
