## CareerPilot Salone - Standardized Design System

### Applied Standards:
1. **Background**: Clean white (#FFFFFF)
2. **Page Headers**: White cards with subtle borders, no images/gradients
3. **Typography**: Inter font only
4. **Colors**: 
   - Green-blue gradient ONLY for: page headings, CTAs
   - Body text: Dark gray (#0B1F3A)
   - Secondary text: Slate gray
5. **Cards**: White background, border-slate-100, shadow-sm
6. **Icons**: Simple, in light gray boxes
7. **No decorative elements**: No background images, no gradient overlays
